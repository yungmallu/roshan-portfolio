About

I’m a music producer, rapper, and mix engineer creating in both rap (melodic, traditional, trap) and EDM. I started rapping in 2018 and released my first track the same year. Whether it’s writing lyrics, building a beat, or mixing for clarity and vibe, music is always about expression — and I’m here for all of it.
